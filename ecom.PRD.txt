## 1. Overview

### 1.1 Purpose
This PRD defines the **functional, non-functional, and technical requirements** for the **Blush by Mounika** e-commerce platform and **admin management system**.  
The goal is to create a premium, mobile-first, scalable online shopping experience for customers and an efficient back-office tool for internal operations.

### 1.2 Scope
- **E-commerce Frontend (Customer Site)**
  - Product catalog (ethnic wear, lehengas, dresses, kids wear, etc.)
  - Product detail, cart, checkout, payments, shipping, and order tracking
  - Custom / made-to-order flow
  - Customer account, wishlist, and reviews
  - SEO, promotions, and mobile-first UX

- **Admin Dashboard (Back Office)**
  - Product & category management
  - Order, inventory, and customer management
  - CMS for banners, blogs, and promotional content
  - Analytics, reporting, and user access control

### 4.2 Admin System

#### 4.2.1 Authentication & Roles
- Secure login (2FA optional).
- Roles: Admin, Merchandiser, Order Manager, Marketing, Finance.
- Role-based access control (RBAC).
- Audit logs for critical actions.

#### 4.2.2 Product Management
- Add/edit/delete products:
  - Fields: Name, SKU, Description, Category, Price, Sale Price, Images, Inventory, Fabric, Size, Color, Tags, Made-to-Order flag.
- Bulk upload (CSV/Excel).
- Manage categories, collections, attributes.
- Media library for product images.
- Draft ‚Üí Publish workflow.

#### 4.2.3 Inventory Management
- Track stock per SKU.
- Low-stock alerts.
- View inventory valuation (cost √ó quantity).
- Update inventory manually or via CSV.

#### 4.2.4 Orders Management
- Orders list: filter by status/date/payment method.
- Order detail view with:
  - Customer info
  - Items
  - Status history
  - Payment & shipping details
- Update status: `Pending ‚Üí Processing ‚Üí Shipped ‚Üí Delivered`.
- Courier API integration for label generation & tracking.
- Handle returns, refunds, and exchanges.

#### 4.2.5 Customer Management
- View all customers with filters.
- Access purchase history, total spend.
- Add notes/tags for segmentation.
- Manage addresses and opt-ins.

#### 4.2.6 Marketing & CMS
- Homepage banners (upload, schedule, enable/disable).
- Manage promotions (discount codes, campaigns).
- Blog management (create/edit/delete posts).
- SEO settings for each page/product.
- Newsletter subscriber list (export/integrate).

#### 4.2.7 Reporting & Analytics
- Dashboard with:
  - Total Sales, Orders, Avg Order Value, Returns.
  - Revenue by product/category.
  - Top-selling products.
  - Customer acquisition summary.
- Export CSV/PDF.
- Daily automated reports to admin email.

#### 4.2.8 Settings & Integrations
- Store info (currency, time zone, tax rules, contact).
- Payment gateways (Razorpay, Stripe, PayU).
- Shipping providers (Delhivery, Shiprocket).
- Notification services (SendGrid, Twilio, WhatsApp Cloud API).
- Backup and restore configuration.
